Barkdoku — Support

Cozy Queens/Star-Battle logic puzzles with dogs.

How to play

Place exactly one dog in every color region, every row and every column — and no two dogs may ever touch, not even diagonally. Tap once for an X, tap the X again to place a dog. Drag across cells to mark several X at once. Every puzzle has exactly one solution and can always be solved by pure logic — the hint button shows the next forced move and explains why.

FAQ

Do I need an account? No. Barkdoku has no accounts, works 100% offline and collects no data.

What does Premium include? All dog breeds instantly plus unlimited hints. One-time purchase, family-shareable. Restore it any time via Settings → Restore purchases.

My progress is missing on a new device. Progress syncs through iCloud automatically when iCloud Drive is enabled on both devices.

The colors are hard to distinguish. Enable Settings → “Region patterns (colorblind aid)” for an additional pattern on every color.
